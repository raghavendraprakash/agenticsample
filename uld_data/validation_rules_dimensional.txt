DIMENSIONAL CONSTRAINT VALIDATION RULES
========================================

RULE 001: Maximum Single Piece Dimensions
- No single piece shall exceed ULD internal dimensions
- Length: Must not exceed ULD internal length
- Width: Must not exceed ULD internal width
- Height: Must not exceed ULD internal height + overhang allowance
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE 002: Overhang Limits - AKE/LD3
- Top overhang: Maximum 5 cm
- Side overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Front/Rear overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE 003: Overhang Limits - AAA/LD7
- Top overhang: Maximum 5 cm
- Side overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Front/Rear overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE 004: Overhang Limits - AMA/LD9
- Top overhang: Maximum 5 cm
- Side overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Front/Rear overhang: 0 cm (not permitted)
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE 005: Weight Distribution
- Center of gravity must be within middle 60% of ULD length
- Maximum weight concentration: 500 kg per square meter
- Violation Level: High

RULE 006: Stacking Height
- Total stacked height must not exceed ULD internal height + overhang
- Heavy items must be on bottom
- Violation Level: High

RULE 007: Volume Utilization
- Minimum utilization: 60% for standard cargo
- Maximum utilization: 95% (allow for securing)
- Violation Level: Medium

RULE 008: Piece Count Limits
- AKE/LD3: Maximum 15 pieces
- AAA/LD7: Maximum 25 pieces
- AMA/LD9: Maximum 35 pieces
- Violation Level: Medium

RULE 009: Mixed Commodity Restrictions
- Dangerous goods must be segregated
- Live animals cannot be mixed with other cargo
- Perishables must be grouped together
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE 010: Fragile Cargo Placement
- Fragile items must be on top layer
- No heavy items above fragile cargo
- Minimum 10 cm clearance from ULD walls
- Violation Level: High
