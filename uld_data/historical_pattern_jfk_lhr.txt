HISTORICAL LOADING PATTERN ANALYSIS
====================================

Route: JFK (New York) to LHR (London)
Aircraft Type: B777-300ER
Analysis Period: Last 90 days
Sample Size: 180 flights

AVERAGE LOAD CHARACTERISTICS:
Total ULDs per Flight: 24-28 (out of 32 positions)
Average Cargo Weight: 14,200 kg (77% of capacity)
Average Volume Utilization: 72%

ULD TYPE DISTRIBUTION:
AKE (LD3): 65% of positions
AAA (LD7): 25% of positions
AKN (LD8): 8% of positions
Other: 2% of positions

CARGO MIX PATTERNS:
General Cargo: 55%
Electronics: 20%
Perishables: 12%
Heavy Machinery: 8%
Other: 5%

POSITION UTILIZATION PATTERNS:
Forward Positions (F1-F8): 95% utilization
- Preferred for priority and perishable cargo
- Average weight: 620 kg per position

Aft Positions (A1-A12): 88% utilization
- Mixed cargo types
- Average weight: 580 kg per position

Rear Positions (R1-R4): 65% utilization
- Often used for last-minute bookings
- Average weight: 450 kg per position

COMMON LOADING SEQUENCES:
1. Heavy cargo in aft positions first
2. Perishables in forward climate-controlled positions
3. General cargo fills remaining positions
4. Last-minute cargo in rear positions

Aft: 25% of total weight
