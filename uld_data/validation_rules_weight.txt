STRUCTURAL WEIGHT VALIDATION RULES
===================================

RULE W001: ULD Maximum Gross Weight
- Total weight (cargo + tare) must not exceed ULD max gross weight
- AKE/LD3: 1588 kg maximum
- AAA/LD7: 4626 kg maximum
- AKN/LD8: 2449 kg maximum
- AAP/LD6: 3176 kg maximum
- AMA/LD9: 6804 kg maximum
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE W002: Aircraft Position Weight Limit
- ULD weight must not exceed position structural limit
- B777: 1200 kg per position
- A350: 1150 kg per position
- B747: 1500 kg (main), 1200 kg (lower)
- B767: 1100 kg per position
- A330: 1150 kg per position
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE W003: Floor Loading Limit
- Weight per square meter must not exceed floor limit
- B777: 732 kg/m²
- A350: 710 kg/m²
- B747: 1464 kg/m² (main), 732 kg/m² (lower)
- B767: 683 kg/m²
- A330: 710 kg/m²
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE W004: Total Aircraft Cargo Weight
- Sum of all ULD weights must not exceed aircraft max cargo capacity
- Includes tare weight of all ULDs
- Violation Level: Critical

RULE W005: Weight Balance
- Forward/Aft weight distribution must be within 5% of target
- Left/Right weight distribution must be within 2% of target
- Violation Level: High

RULE W006: Heavy Cargo Concentration
- No more than 3 consecutive heavy ULDs (>1000 kg) in same row
- Minimum 1 lighter ULD between heavy concentrations
- Violation Level: Medium

RULE W007: Single Piece Weight Limit
- No single piece should exceed 70% of ULD max net weight
- Facilitates handling and distribution
- Violation Level: Medium
